BMW M3 E46

History & Legacy

The BMW M3 E46, produced from 2000 to 2006, is widely regarded as one of the best M3s ever made. With its naturally aspirated 3.2L inline-six engine producing 333 hp, it blends raw power with sharp handling and classic styling. It’s a legend among car enthusiasts, renowned for its balance, precision, and driving pleasure.

Key Specifications

Engine

3.2L I6 S54 naturally aspirated

Power

333 hp @ 7,900 rpm

Torque

365 Nm (269 lb-ft) @ 4,900 rpm

0-100 km/h

4.8 seconds

Top Speed

250 km/h (electronically limited)

Transmission

6-speed manual / SMG II (sequential manual)
